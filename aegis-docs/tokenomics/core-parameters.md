# Core Parameters

The AEGIS token has been designed with transparency and fairness as its guiding principles. The token structure fundamentally aligns with the security standards that the Aegis platform advocates.

### Token Specifications
* **Token Name:** Aegis
* **Ticker:** AEGIS
* **Network:** Solana
* **Total Supply:** 1,000,000,000 (1 Billion)
* **Decimals:** 6

### Zero Tax Policy
AEGIS maintains a permanent zero-tax policy. There is no buy tax, no sell tax, and no hidden fees extracted during the transaction lifecycle. This policy is enforced strictly at the smart contract level.

### Renounced Authorities
To guarantee the integrity of the token supply and protect token holders, critical smart contract authorities are renounced immediately at launch:
* **Mint Authority Renounced:** The total supply is permanently fixed at one billion tokens. No new tokens can ever be created.
* **Freeze Authority Renounced:** No entity, including the Aegis development team, retains the ability to freeze any holder's token account.

### Supply Distribution
The token supply is allocated to support long-term ecosystem growth and liquidity:
* **70% (700,000,000):** Public Launch and Liquidity Provision
* **15% (150,000,000):** Ecosystem and Strategic Partnerships
* **10% (100,000,000):** Team and Development (Subject to a 12-month vesting schedule with a 3-month cliff)
* **5% (50,000,000):** Community Rewards and Incentives
